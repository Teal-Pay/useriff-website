Payment Transactions: What You Should Know

Crypto Transactions Are Final

Once you send or exchange crypto (including conversions between crypto and fiat), those transactions cannot be reversed or canceled. Always double-check recipient addresses and amounts before you confirm.

ACH Transfers (U.S. Bank Transfers)

ACH payments are not instantaneous and may take 3-5 business days to process.

ACH payments can be reversed in certain cases (such as unauthorized transactions or errors) under federal Regulation E. Contact us immediately if you see an error or suspect fraud.

By initiating an ACH transfer, you authorize Gemm (via Cybrid) to debit or credit your linked bank account using the Automated Clearing House (ACH) network.

All ACH transactions are subject to applicable laws and NACHA Operating Rules.

If an ACH debit is returned due to insufficient funds or other reasons, you may be charged a returned payment fee.

For more information about ACH limits and minimums, see Walapay Terms of Service.

Wire Transfers

Wire transfers may have higher limits but also carry fees and processing times. Once a wire is sent, it typically cannot be reversed. Double-check all wire details before confirming.
